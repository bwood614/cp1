About Scripture Search

Scripture Search is a graphic tool that can be used to understand words in the standard works of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints in a new way.

Words can be analyzed in groups based on the work that they appear in as well as across different works. Realtions between words can also be visualized.

The works that can be analyzed includin the Old Testament, the New Testament, the Book of Mormon, the Doctrine and Covenants and The Pearl of Great Price.
